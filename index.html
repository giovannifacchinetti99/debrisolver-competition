Project Overview

Our work analyzes the 2024 Long March 6A fragmentation event (700+ fragments) using more than 500,000 Conjunction Data Messages to identify operational criticalities in current Space Traffic Management frameworks.

The key innovation is the STM-ConOps Score, a composite metric enabling risk-based, proportionate regulation by combining technical, governance, and mission-level factors.
