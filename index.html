Developed for the DebriSolver Competition organized by the Saudi Space Agency and presented at the Space Debris Conference 2026 in Riyadh.

The Challenge

Orbital congestion and fragmentation events threaten the sustainability of space operations. The 2024 Long March 6A breakup generated more than 700 fragments, creating recurring high-risk conjunctions with active missions.

Our Solution

Using over 500,000 CDMs, we reconstruct debris evolution and classify conjunctions. The STM-ConOps Score translates technical risk into actionable, proportionate STM guidance.
