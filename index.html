The Challenge

Satellites are increasingly exposed to collision risk due to orbital congestion, mega-constellations, and fragmentation events. In 2024 alone, the Long March 6A breakup generated over 700 fragments threatening critical space infrastructure.

Our Solution

We analyze more than 500,000 Conjunction Data Messages to reconstruct debris-cloud evolution and identify operational risk drivers. Our key innovation is the STM-ConOps Score, enabling proportionate, risk-based STM regulation.
